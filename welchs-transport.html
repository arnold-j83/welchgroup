Welch's Transport - Dependable, high-quality service with a competitive edge...

The Welch's Transport haulage fleet is predominantly made up with MAN, ERF and DAF vehicles. For maximum general haulage flexibility it includes transit vans, 7.5 tonne curtain-siders with tail-lift, 18 tonne curtain-siders with tail-lift and 44 tonne curtain-sided and flat-bed artics, each with air-ride suspension to protect your freight. The Welch's Transport haulage fleet is maintained in-house with a stringent policy of regular renewal, in order to meet or exceed current legislative and environmental standards, to provide a pleasant working environment for our drivers and to ensure a professional image at delivery point.

The efficient utilisation of the Welch's Transport haulage fleet is backed up with more than 120 years of collective operational experience across the depots, further enhanced by software which allows depot-wide visibility of all freight movements on a day-to-day basis.

Our Mission - Standards our Business Partners Should Expect from Welch's

  1. We will deal with our customers, suppliers and other partners on every occasion in a straight forward, open and honest manner.
  2. Staff will present themselves in suitable business attire wearing company uniforms and Personal Protective Equipment (P.P.E.) if required to do so. Variations of the company uniform are not acceptable.
  3. Staff will conduct themselves on all occasions in a professional, businesslike manner and with the aim of providing consistently high levels of customer service.
  4. Company vehicles will be deployed and operated in a safe and road worthy condition and be kept clean inside and out.
  5. Company premises will be kept clean and tidy with individuals personally responsible for their own work area.
  6. The answering of a ringing telephone will take priority over all other tasks.
  7. Once a member of staff is made aware of an issue with a customer, supplier or other business partner he/she will take ownership of the problem and manage it to a conclusion in a prompt and professional manner.
  8. Communication within the group, with customers, with suppliers and with business partners is vital to the success of our business. We will respond to all communications regardless of how it is received professionally and in a timely manner.

Welch's Transport - Hazardous Freight

Safe distribution of Dangerous Goods

Welch’s Transport joined The Hazchem Network in January 2006 to respond to market demands to provide a safe and high quality method of distributing ADR regulated freight. The strategic acquisition of Dods-Brown Distribution (DBD) strengthened our commitment to this specific market. DBD has now been integrated fully into the Duxford depot which means resources can be better utilised for more efficient deliveries into and collections from Hertfordshire, Cambridgeshire and Essex, for onward delivery around the UK on a next-day or economy basis.

Welch's Transport will accept hazardous freight classes 2, 3, 4, 6.1, 8, 9, 5.1 and selected 5.2 as bagged product, drums or cartons on pallets, IBCs, and polybulks and offer a warehousing service comprising 10,000 square feet. Welch's Transport also operates specialist vehicles for clients where possible.

The Welch Group; a history

Founded in 1934 by brothers Gordon and Jim Welch with one 3 tonne Morris, petrol-driven truck from a small village in South Cambridgeshire, The Welch Group of Companies now employs 165 industry professionals with an annual turnover in excess of £12 million.

From a single depot in Foxton, Cambridgeshire, the company has grown steadily into a nationwide service provider, diversifying into many related industries. From the outset our business ethic has always been to own outright all its vehicles and property so that costs are kept to a minimum in order to offer the most competitive rates possible to the various marketplaces.

Welch's Transport now has depots in Duxford in Cambridgeshire, Henlow in Bedfordshire, Witham in Essex and St Ives, Cambridge. Our Duxford and Henlow depots have a modern, mixed fleet of vehicles and offer a nationwide, high-quality service for items ranging from a single pallet to maximum weight articulated vehicle loads. The Witham depot specialises in delivering dry-powder cement to construction sites from a range of tank vehicles for clients such as Tarmac, Cemex and CPI.

One of Welch's Transport's specialist services is its Contract Distribution Division supplying new, liveried vehicles and drivers in customer-branded uniforms to clients which prefer to either introduce or outsource the management of a vehicle or fleet and driver(s) and the compliance with ever-changing and increasingly stringent O-licence legislation.

Welch's Specialist Movements at St Ives has a fleet of lorry-mounted cranes, heavy forklifts and low loader trailers and works closely with our Crane Hire Division to offer bespoke solutions from complete factory removals to special movements of large, heavy or awkward items. In 2016 it will take delivery of a new, crane trailer which is the only one of its kind in the UK.

During the latter part of the 1980s, Welch's Truck and Van Centres was born out of market demand for a professionally operated commercial garage, servicing local businesses ranging from owner-drivers to large fleet operators. It has a new, flagship site in Duxford and a well-established site in Fengate, Peterborough both of which are franchised Renault dealers but which have the skills and parts stock to service all makes of trucks.
