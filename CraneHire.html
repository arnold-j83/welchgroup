Welch's Crane Hire




Welch's Crane Hire

Based in Cambridgeshire, Welch's Crane Hire operates a selection of cranes ranging from Kato 25 Ton City to a Liebheer LTM 1055.

Our fully CPCS trained operators offer a professional lifting service for Contract Lifts, Crane Hire, Slinger/Signaller and Appointed Person.

We offer site surveys, where we visit your site and advise you on the most cost effective hire to suit your needs. These are carried out free of charge in the local area.

All Contract Lifts & Crane Hires are carried out under CPA terms & conditions.

All equipment is checked and certificated on a twice-yearly basis by an independent insurance company to ensure compliance with Health & Safety regulations.

Established in 1934 Welch’s Crane Hire is one of several businesses belonging to Welch Group Holdings which is independently owned and directed by the Welch family. Based in Cambridgeshire, Welch's Crane Hire operates a selection of cranes ranging from Kato 25 Ton City to a Liebherr LTM 1055.

Welch's Crane Hire Lifting Accessories

Welch's Crane Hire can offer a selection of slings and lifting equipment that can be adapted for most lifts:

  • Brick forks & Safety net
  • Personnel basket. (2 person or 350kg SWL)
  • Fall arrester & Safety harness
  • 1000 litre Roll over muck skips
  • 500 litre Cone concrete skip
  • 1000 litre Boat skips
  • A selection of lifting beams & bars for air conditioning units etc
  • A wide range of soft round slings, Boat slings, span sets, from 1 tonne – 30 tonne & shackles
  • A selection of steel road plates, Nylacast rigging pads
  • We can also arrange temporary roadway (track way) by arrangement
  • Delivery & Collection by arrangement
