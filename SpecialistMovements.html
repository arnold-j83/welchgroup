Welch's Specialist Movements

Established in 1934 Welch’s Specialist Movements is one of several businesses belonging to Welch Group Holdings which is independently owned and directed by the Welch family. Its fleet includes access to heavy forklifts of up to 12 tonnes lifting capacity, versa lift 60/80 with a lift capacity of up to 44,800kg, Tri-Lifter (mobile crane/forklift/gantry) with a capacity of up to 92 tonnes, lorry-mounted cranes with a lift capacity of up to 10 tonnes, stair-climbers, and several low-loaders, including extendable low loaders.

Each of Welch's Transport Ltd depots operates to RHA terms and conditions and is accredited to BS EN ISO9001:2000 which means your goods are protected, insured and cared for.

All equipment is checked and certificated on a twice-yearly basis by an independent insurance company to ensure compliance with Health & Safety regulations.

Welch's Specialist Movements Workshop Services

Here at Welch's Commercials in St Ives we are skilled and equipped with up to date diagnostic technology to carry out maintenance and repairs on all vehicles from trucks and trailers to your work van and personal car. We have a team of 3 people with over 40 years experience within the vehicle and repair industry along with an ADR licence to enable us to collect and service tankers. Our guys understand that whilst your vehicle is off the road it can be costly in time and money, so we are committed to provide you with a quality and efficient service/repair for your vehicles to get you back on the road.

We hold accounts with major suppliers and the backing of Renault & MAN Dealerships as part of The Welch Group.

  • Arrange brake testing and tacho calibration
  • All vehicle maintenance as required
  • Vehicle diagnostic equipment

Welch's Specialist Movements - Projects

Welch's Specialist Movements has now undertaken all types of movement and installation projects, as an example we have listed a few below:

Digital Advertising Sign - Peterborough to Trafford Centre, Manchester,

Removal and Loading of a Digital Advertising Sign

One or our most recent projects has included a digital advertising sign that had to be removed from the build area in the factory and stood up so our client could then add the finishing touches before transporting to Manchester, and installing onto a pre-cast concrete base. The sign was 9m tall and weighed 8000kgs, to stand up we had to use two of our mobile cranes to enable our customer to add the finishing touches before we could then lay it back down and load on our extendable step frame trailer ready for transport to Manchester.

Installation of Sign in Manchester

We had to be onsite at 9pm once the road was closed, we then lifted the sign and stood it up using our 42t/m crane. We then lifted the sign over the hoardings and lowered on to the pre-cast concrete base. The operation took just 2 hours from us arriving to our vehicle departing the site keeping traffic disruption to a minimum. All our customer had to do was plug the sign in and turn it on!
